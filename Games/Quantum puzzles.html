How to Play

Quantum Patterns is a puzzle game that challenges your ability to recognize patterns and think creatively.

Each puzzle presents a sequence of elements following a hidden pattern. Your task is to identify the pattern and select the next element in the sequence.

As you progress, patterns become more complex and may involve:

  • Colors and shapes
  • Mathematical sequences
  • Rotations and reflections
  • Hidden rules that change between levels

Think outside the box! Sometimes the pattern isn't what it first appears to be.
