Cloudflare Setup Guide

Easy step-by-step instructions to create your account and find your credentials

1

Create Your Cloudflare Account

Go to cloudflare.com and click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. Enter your email and create a password. Verify your email address by clicking the link Cloudflare sends you.

üí° Tip: Use a strong password and keep it safe!

2

Navigate to Your Profile

Once logged in, click on your profile icon in the top right corner. From the dropdown menu, select "My Profile". This will take you to your account settings page.

üéØ Look for: A circular icon with your initials or avatar

3

Get Your API Key

In your profile, scroll down to find the "API Tokens" section. Click "View" next to "Global API Key". You'll need to enter your password to reveal it. Click "Copy" to save it to your clipboard.

4

Find Your Account ID

Go back to your Cloudflare dashboard homepage. Select any website (or add one if needed). On the right sidebar under "API", you'll see your "Account ID". Click the copy icon next to it to copy it to your clipboard.
